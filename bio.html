Bio

Amy Pellegrini

Web Application Developer | Pianist | Polymath

I'm a pianist, artist, software developer, and activist for transgender people rights.

Toco el piano desde hace más de 15 años and I have 4+ years of professional experience in software and web applications development.

My talent is not limited only to this fields, también me dedico (altuogh not in a professional manner) to writing, drawing, and the creation of audiovisual educational/documental material.

Given this plurality of interests in different disciplines, las cuales me esfuerzo por integrar y potenciar entre sí, es que I consider myself a polymath woman.

When I was 28 de edad I discovered and embraced my transgender condition, got out of the closet, y desde ese momento he trabajado intensamente para interiorizarme sobre la situación general de las personas trans, con la firme intención de lograr un cambio que no solo permita una sana inclusión de la comunidad trans, sino una mayor libertad para todo ser humano en su condición de ser.

Denuonce, demistify, deconstruct, and propose are the 4 axis of my work as an artist and activist, 4 axis which I consider essential to engage a cultural reform which in time echoes itself in the form of social transformation.
