PIANO BAR SHOW & Little Wooden People EXPO

Enjoy an artistic evening with traditional Piano Bar music by pianist Amy Pellegrini, while you enjoy the beautiful story and artwork of Irma Pellegrini, in a special event where mother & daughter share their story and creative gifts given by nature.

Entrance is free, food & drinks (non-alcoholic) for sale by the Bow Belly CANTEEN.

We will have some wine & beers to share, any other alcoholic beverage may be available on request (please ask before the event) or you can bring it yourself and the CANTEEN personnel will be happy to serve it.

Feel free to visit Irma Pellegrini's website: ipellegrini.com/

Where to find us:

CANTEEN, Trinity Wharf, Harrington Way, London SE18 5NR.

On the riverfront, turn off warspite road into Harrington way, pass through the site gates and turn immediately right to the river. See more at: http://www.secondfloor.co.uk/canteen/default.aspx#sthash.ghOZRBdF.dpuf
